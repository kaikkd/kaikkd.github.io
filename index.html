About Me

I build practical AI systems for coding, evaluation, and multimodal understanding. My current work focuses on making agent workflows stable, measurable, and useful in real engineering environments.

I am currently participating in the UC San Diego CSE CS75 project. I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Forestry University.

Research Interests

  • Agent systems: coding agents, tool-use workflows, multi-turn state management, and self-correction.
  • Model evaluation: function-calling benchmarks, sandboxed execution, factuality checks, and LLM/VLM metrics.
  • Applied AI: RAG systems, multimodal recognition, remote sensing, and domain adaptation.
